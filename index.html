process of purchasing a product

Concert: Go to JYP Web or other tick service website -> Search through date or specific singer -> Purchase the ticket Audition process: Sign-In to JYP Web -> Application -> Upload Required Files -> Successful Field of Audition: Vocal/Dance/Act/Model/Mc/VJ
